Utica Venue Corridor

Office of the Brooklym Borough President (OBBP) selected the Utica Avenue Corridor study as a capstone project to understand the opportunities along the corridor from Eastern Parkway to Avenue I, with a quater mile buffer from the corridor, in concert with the extension of subway line 4. Identifying the potential for reactivating parking and vacant lots was a key component of the project.As a first step towards this analysis, the Interactivity on this map gives information about the built FAR for parking garages,exisitng lot area and zoning for the lots. This information can be useful to further identify the built and open space uses that can be proposed for reactivating the lots.

Cick on the parking/vacant lots to get more info
